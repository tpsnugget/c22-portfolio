My name is Michael Giebner

I am a junior, full-stack web developer intent on starting my next career. My last career was almost 28-years in the US Air Force. Over my career I held five majorly different jobs: electronic technical, flight engineer on a C-141B Starlifter, flight test engineer, program manager and Air Force ROTC instructor. I flew for about half my career and ended up with somewhere around 1,600 flight hours in 20+ different aircraft.

I enjoy full-stack development immensely and am looking forward to securing a position with a great company. My preference would be to work on a team with more senior developers so that I can be mentored into a better, more seasoned developer. However, I am also open to contract or part-time work as well. I am a good team player and will be a productive asset for the organization that gives me a chance.

I am a little different (or a lot different) than your average junior developer. My wealth of other experiences will be beneficial in the workplace as well. The bottom line is that as a retired military officer I do not need a job, but I do want one. I like being productive and am excited about the prospect of landing with a good company.
